## Executive Summary

**Aukcije.ba** is a regional auction platform for the Balkan market (Bosnia & Herzegovina, Croatia, Serbia, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Slovenia).

### Key Highlights

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Market Size** | 19M population, 15M internet users |
| **Revenue Model** | Commissions (60%), Subscriptions (20%), Featured Listings (10%), Payments (5%), Ads (5%) |
| **Break-Even** | Month 10-12 |
| **Year 1 Revenue** | 153,000 BAM (~78,000 EUR) |
| **Year 3 Revenue** | 8,520,000 BAM (~4,350,000 EUR) |
| **Year 3 Margin** | 51% |
| **Seed Funding** | 380,000 BAM (~194,000 EUR) for 20% equity |

### Investment Thesis

✅ **Large Market:** 19M population, 15M internet users  
✅ **First-Mover Advantage:** Only regional auction platform with escrow  
✅ **Strong Unit Economics:** LTV:CAC of 5:1 by Year 1  
✅ **Scalable Model:** 85% gross margins at scale  
✅ **Technical Foundation:** 96% complete

## Risk Analysis

### Market Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Low adoption | Medium | High | Aggressive marketing, referral incentives (10 BAM credit), free Premium tier for early adopters |
| Competitor response | High | Medium | Focus on UX, lower fees (3-8% vs 10%), better support, escrow protection |
| Market saturation | Low | Medium | Focus on underserved niches (collectibles, vehicles), cross-border trade |

### Operational Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Fraud/Shill bidding | Medium | High | AI detection algorithms, manual review, user reporting, KYC verification |
| Payment failures | Low | Medium | Multiple gateways (Stripe, Monri, CorvusPay), wallet fallback, retry logic |
| Server downtime | Low | High | 99.9% SLA, auto-scaling, redundant infrastructure, monitoring |
| Data breach | Low | High | Encryption, regular security audits, GDPR compliance, cyber insurance |

### Financial Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Cash flow shortage | Medium | High | 6-month runway, milestone-based funding, cost control, early revenue focus |
| Currency fluctuation | Low | Low | Multi-currency support, natural hedging (revenue/costs in same currency) |
| Payment processor issues | Low | Medium | Multiple processors, direct bank integration, wallet system |

### Legal/Compliance Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| GDPR violation | Low | High | Compliance by design, DPO appointment, regular audits, user consent management |
| Payment license | Medium | High | Partner with licensed processors initially, apply for e-money license |
| Consumer protection | Low | Medium | Clear Terms of Service, dispute resolution, escrow protection |
| Tax compliance | Low | Medium | Automated VAT calculation, tax reporting, local entity in each market |
