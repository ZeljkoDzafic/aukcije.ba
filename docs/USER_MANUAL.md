## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit [aukcije.ba](https://aukcije.ba)
2. Click "Registruj se" (Register) in the top right
3. Choose account type:
   - **Buyer** - For purchasing items
   - **Seller** - For listing items (requires KYC verification)
4. Fill in your details:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Phone number
5. Click "Registruj se"
6. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox

### Account Verification (KYC)

**Required for sellers:**

1. Go to Profile → KYC Verification
2. **Level 1:** Email verification (automatic)
3. **Level 2:** Phone verification
   - Enter your phone number
   - Receive SMS with code
   - Enter the code to verify
4. **Level 3:** Document verification
   - Upload ID card or passport
   - Upload proof of address
   - Wait for admin approval (24-48 hours)

### Setting Up Your Profile

1. Go to Profile → Settings
2. Add profile picture
3. Set notification preferences
4. Add shipping addresses
5. Connect payment methods

## Buying Guide

### Finding Items

**Search:**
1. Use the search bar at the top
2. Enter keywords (e.g., "Samsung", "iPhone")
3. Press Enter or click search icon

**Browse Categories:**
1. Click "Kategorije" (Categories)
2. Select a category (e.g., Elektronika, Vozila)
3. Browse subcategories

**Filters:**
- Price range (min - max)
- Condition (New, Used, Refurbished)
- Location (City, Country)
- Auction type (Standard, Buy Now)
- Sort by: Ending soon, Price low→high, Price high→low, Newest

### Understanding Auction Pages

**Auction Information:**
- Current price
- Number of bids
- Time remaining
- Seller information and rating
- Item description and photos
- Shipping options

### Placing a Bid

1. **Manual Bidding:**
   - Enter your bid amount
   - Click "LICITIRAJ" (Place Bid)
   - Confirm your bid

2. **Proxy Bidding (Auto-bid):**
   - Check "Proxy bid" checkbox
   - Enter your maximum bid
   - System automatically bids for you up to your maximum
   - You stay winning until someone exceeds your max

3. **Buy Now:**
   - Click "Kupi odmah" (Buy Now)
   - Confirm purchase
   - Proceed to payment

### Winning an Auction

**If you win:**
1. You'll receive email notification
2. Go to "Moje narudžbe" (My Orders)
3. Complete payment within 3 days
4. Wait for seller to ship
5. Confirm delivery
6. Leave rating for seller

**If you're outbid:**
1. You'll receive notification
2. Go to auction page
3. Place higher bid or use proxy bid

### Payment Options

- **Wallet:** Instant payment from your Aukcije wallet
- **Credit/Debit Card:** Stripe, Monri, CorvusPay
- **Bank Transfer:** Direct bank transfer

## Selling Guide

### Creating an Auction

1. Click "Prodaj" (Sell) or go to Seller Dashboard
2. Click "Kreiraj aukciju" (Create Auction)
3. **Step 1: Basic Info**
   - Title (clear and descriptive)
   - Category
   - Condition (New, Used, etc.)
4. **Step 2: Description**
   - Detailed item description
   - Include any defects or issues
   - Be honest and accurate
5. **Step 3: Photos**
   - Upload up to 10 photos
   - Use good lighting
   - Show all angles and any damage
6. **Step 4: Pricing**
   - Start price (minimum bid)
   - Reserve price (optional minimum)
   - Buy Now price (optional)
7. **Step 5: Shipping**
   - Select shipping methods
   - Set shipping cost
   - Specify handling time
8. **Step 6: Review & Publish**
   - Review all information
   - Click "Objavi aukciju" (Publish)

### Auction Duration Options

- 3 days
- 5 days
- 7 days (recommended)
- 10 days

### Managing Your Auctions

**Active Auctions:**
- View current bids
- Respond to buyer questions
- Monitor watch count

**Ended Auctions:**
- View winning bidder
- Send invoice
- Mark as shipped
- Leave rating for buyer

### Seller Fees

|------|-------------|------------|---------------|
| Free | 0 BAM | 8% | 5 auctions/month |
| Premium | 29 BAM | 5% | 50 auctions/month |

### Best Practices for Sellers

**Photos:**

**Description:**
- Be detailed and honest
- Include dimensions

- Specify what's included

**Pricing:**

## Payment & Wallet

### Setting Up Wallet

1. Go to "Novčanik" (Wallet)
2. Click "Aktiviraj novčanik"
3. Accept terms
4. Wallet is ready to use

### Depositing Funds

1. Go to Wallet → Deposit
2. Enter amount (minimum 10 BAM)
3. Select payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Bank Transfer
4. Complete payment
5. Funds appear instantly (card) or 1-3 days (bank)

### Withdrawing Funds

1. Go to Wallet → Withdraw
2. Enter amount (minimum 20 BAM)
3. Enter bank account details
4. Confirm withdrawal
5. Funds arrive in 1-3 business days

### Escrow Protection

**How it works:**
1. Buyer pays → funds held in escrow
2. Seller ships item
3. Buyer confirms delivery
4. Funds released to seller (minus commission)

**Protection for buyers:**
- Funds held until you confirm delivery
- 14-day window to report issues
- Full refund if item not as described

**Protection for sellers:**
- Guaranteed payment
- Protection from chargebacks
- Automatic release after 14 days

## Shipping & Delivery

### Shipping Options

**EuroExpress:**
- Delivery: 1-2 days (BiH)
- Tracking included
- Insurance included

**PostExpress:**
- Delivery: 2-3 days (Croatia)
- Tracking included
- Insurance included

**BH Pošta:**
- Delivery: 2-4 days (BiH)
- Tracking included

### Shipping Costs

Calculated based on:
- Package weight
- Dimensions
- Destination
- Shipping speed

### Tracking Your Package

1. Go to "Moje narudžbe" (My Orders)
2. Click on order
3. Click "Prati pošiljku" (Track Package)
4. Enter tracking number on courier website

### Delivery Confirmation

**As buyer:**
1. Inspect package upon delivery
2. Check item condition
3. Go to "Moje narudžbe"
4. Click "Potvrdi dostavu" (Confirm Delivery)
5. Leave rating for seller

**If item is damaged:**
1. Take photos immediately
2. Contact seller within 24 hours
3. Open dispute if not resolved
4. Provide evidence (photos, description)

## Dispute Resolution

### Opening a Dispute

**When to open:**
- Item not received
- Item not as described
- Item damaged
- Wrong item sent

**How to open:**
1. Go to "Moje narudžbe"
2. Click on order
3. Click "Otvori spor" (Open Dispute)
4. Select reason
5. Provide description
6. Upload evidence (photos, messages)

### Dispute Process

**Timeline:**
1. **Day 1:** Dispute opened
2. **Day 1-2:** Seller responds
3. **Day 3-5:** Admin reviews (if escalated)
4. **Day 5:** Resolution issued

**Possible Resolutions:**
- Full refund to buyer
- Partial refund
- Release payment to seller
- Return item for refund

### Tips for Dispute Resolution

**For buyers:**
- Communicate with seller first
- Provide clear evidence
- Be reasonable in expectations
- Respond promptly to requests

**For sellers:**
- Respond within 48 hours
- Provide tracking information
- Be professional
- Offer fair solutions

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is registration free?**
A: Yes, registration is free. Sellers can list 5 auctions free per month.

**Q: How do I verify my account?**
A: Email verification is automatic. Phone and document verification are in Profile → KYC.

**Q: Can I cancel a bid?**
A: Bids are binding. Contact seller if you made an error.

### Buying Questions

**Q: What happens if I'm outbid?**
A: You'll receive notification. You can place a higher bid.

**Q: How long do I have to pay?**
A: 3 days from auction end. After that, order may be cancelled.

**Q: What if I don't receive my item?**
A: Open a dispute within 7 days of expected delivery.

### Selling Questions

**Q: How long until I receive payment?**
A: After buyer confirms delivery, or automatically after 14 days.

**Q: Can I end auction early?**
A: Only if there are no bids. Contact support for assistance.

**Q: What if item doesn't sell?**
A: You can relist for free or revise the listing.

### Payment Questions

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, we use encrypted payment processors (Stripe, Monri, CorvusPay).

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refunds are processed through dispute resolution or mutual agreement.

**Q: Are there withdrawal fees?**
A: No, withdrawals are free.
