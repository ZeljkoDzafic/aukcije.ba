# Privacy Policy (Politika Privatnosti)

**Last Updated:** March 2026  
**Effective Date:** [Date]

## 1. Introduction

Aukcije.ba ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our auction platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

- **Identity Data:** Name, username, date of birth
- **Contact Data:** Email address, phone number, address
- **Financial Data:** Bank account details, payment card information (processed by third parties)
- **Transaction Data:** Details about payments and transactions
- **KYC Data:** Identity documents, verification photos (for sellers)
- **Profile Data:** Profile picture, preferences, feedback, ratings

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Data

- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, device information
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent, click patterns
- **Location Data:** General location based on IP address

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

1. **Service Delivery:** To operate the auction platform
2. **Transaction Processing:** To process payments and orders
3. **Verification:** KYC/AML compliance for sellers
4. **Communication:** To send notifications about auctions, bids, orders
5. **Security:** To prevent fraud and ensure platform security
6. **Analytics:** To improve our services
7. **Legal Compliance:** To comply with applicable laws

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

- **Contract:** Processing necessary to fulfill our contract with you
- **Consent:** Where you have given explicit consent
- **Legal Obligation:** Where required by law
- **Legitimate Interest:** For our legitimate business interests

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

- **Payment Processors:** Stripe, Monri, CorvusPay
- **Shipping Partners:** EuroExpress, PostExpress, BH Pošta
- **Service Providers:** Hosting, email, analytics providers
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by law
- **Business Transfers:** In case of merger or acquisition

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data for:

- **Active Accounts:** As long as your account is active
- **Transaction Records:** 7 years (legal requirement)
- **KYC Documents:** 5 years after verification
- **Deleted Accounts:** 30 days (cooling period)
- **Server Logs:** 90 days

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request a copy of your data
2. **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
3. **Erasure:** Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
4. **Portability:** Receive your data in machine-readable format
5. **Objection:** Object to processing
6. **Restriction:** Request restriction of processing
7. **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time

## 8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- **Access Control:** Role-based access control
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring
- **Backups:** Daily encrypted backups

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to:

- **EU/EEA:** Within European Economic Area
- **Adequate Countries:** Countries with adequacy decisions
- **Safeguards:** Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)

## 10. Cookies

We use:

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for platform functionality
- **Analytics Cookies:** To understand usage patterns
- **Marketing Cookies:** For targeted advertising (with consent)

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

## 11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
